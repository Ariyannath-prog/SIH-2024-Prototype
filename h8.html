ALOE VERA


PHOTOS:


Botanical Name: Aloe barbadensis

Common name: Gwar patha / Ghrit kumari

Aloe vera thrives in arid, semi-desert environments with well-drained soil and ample sunlight. Native to the Arabian Peninsula, it adapts well to warm, dry climates but can be cultivated in similar conditions worldwide. lt is cultivated by planting offsets or pups in well-drained soil with good sunlight. It requires minimal watering and thrives in warm climates. Regularly ensuring the soil isn't too moist helps prevent root rot and promotes healthy growth. It is used for its soothing properties in treating burns, cuts, and skin irritations. It aids in wound healing, moisturizes skin, and can alleviate digestive issues like constipation when consumed. Its anti-inflammatation and antimicrobialeffects enhance overall health.
